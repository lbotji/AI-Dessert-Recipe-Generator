AI Dessert Recipe Generator

Ingredients 250g frozen mixed berry
250g Greek yogurt
honey or agave syrup

Method step 1 Blend berries, yogurt and honey or agave syrup in a food processor for 20 seconds, until it comes together to a smooth ice-cream texture. Scoop into bowls and serve.
